Poster Target (Carton's app) Privacy rules

Android Camera permission

The camera is only used to analyze the flow of images. The analyze is done with an offline technology from Vuforia. It allows the application to detect specific pictures (such as the posters). In any case, at no time the pictures or any information analyze from the camera is recorded or saved somewhere, neither on the phone, nor on the cloud on private servers. You should not take pictures of private sensible information about you or any other person without given permission.
